What is AstroMind?

AstroMind is an AI-driven space psychology simulator where you step into Mission Control and navigate the unseen challenges of deep space. Astronauts aren’t just explorers—they're humans with unique behaviors, emotions, and reactions. Your mission? Analyze crew dynamics, solve unpredictable crises, and make critical decisions that could mean survival or failure. Blending AI, psychology, and space exploration, AstroMind reveals what’s usually invisible, turning the unknown into insight. Are you ready to think like an astronaut, decide like a leader, and explore beyond the stars?
